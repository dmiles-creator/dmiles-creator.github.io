About

AI and Data Governance at enterprise scale.

I sit at the intersection of AI governance and data strategy — partnering with C-suite leaders and Audit Committees to architect the policies, data architectures, and risk frameworks that turn complex regulatory landscapes into competitive advantages.

My track record spans Fortune 100 global brands including Apple, Capital One, Coca-Cola, and Bristol-Myers Squibb, where I built enterprise-scale programs in TPRM, data governance, and AI risk management. Before my current role leading AI governance at a hyper-growth AI technology company, I spent nearly a decade at PwC advising the world’s most complex organizations.

I build programs that enable responsible AI deployment — not programs that slow innovation down. That distinction is why organizations trust me to architect governance frameworks at the executive level.
